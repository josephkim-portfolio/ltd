About

My name is Joseph Kim, and I am a student in the Learning Technologies and Design program at the University of Missouri. My educational goals aim to develop my competencies in the field of instructional design for higher education and in workplace learning and development settings. I aspire to collaborate with subject matter experts to develop meaningful and creative learning experiences. This includes creating engaging content, scaffolded activities, and aligned assessments. Additionally, I seek to utilize my graphic design, video editing, and Adobe Captivate skills to enhance the multimedia aspects of online learning.
