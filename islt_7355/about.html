About

My name is Joseph Kim, and I am a student in the Information Science and Learning Technologies program at the University of Missouri.

My educational goals seek to develop my competencies in the field of instructional design in higher education. I aspire to collaborate with instructors to develop meaningful and creative online learning experiences for students. This includes helping teachers create engaging content, scaffolded activities, and aligned assessments. Additionally, I seek to utilize my graphic design, video editing, and Adobe Captivate skills to enhance the multimedia aspects of online learning.
