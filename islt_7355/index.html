Welcome!

This portfolio showcases selected artifacts developed throughout my Educational Specialist in the Information Science and Learning Technologies (ISLT) program at the University of Missouri. Graduates of the program must demonstrate three competencies by the end of their coursework. For each competency, one relevant artifact shall be showcased, with an accompanying reflection.

To learn more about my professional goals and education, visit my About page. The Projects page will showcase artifacts I have crafted throughout my ISLT program and how they align with the program’s competencies. The Reflection page will chronicle my career journey to the field of instructional design for higher education. Please feel free to reach me via my Contact page.
